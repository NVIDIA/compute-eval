# ComputeEval Engineering Diaries

A periodic series of technical reports from the ComputeEval team at NVIDIA.
Less formal than a research paper and more substantive than release notes —
each volume covers what we've been building, what we've learned, and where
we're headed.

## Volumes

- **Vol 1 — April 2026** — *Benchmarking LLM-Generated GPU Code: Results, Lessons, and the Road Ahead.*
  Covers the work behind the 2025.3 and 2026.1 releases: the benchmark roughly tripling in size,
  the addition of GPU performance measurement alongside correctness, current leaderboard results,
  early results on net-new knowledge, and a preview of the move to fully agentic evaluation.
  [PDF](ComputeEval_Engineering_Diary_Vol1.pdf)
